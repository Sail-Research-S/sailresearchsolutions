FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our research and publication support services.

Do you guarantee publication?

No. We do not guarantee journal acceptance, publication or indexing. Journal decisions are made by editors and peer reviewers.

Can you prepare a manuscript from my data?

Yes, we can support manuscript preparation if the data are genuine, ethically collected and provided by the client with appropriate permissions.

Do you provide thesis support?

Yes. We support thesis structuring, chapter refinement, tables, figures, discussion, conclusion and formatting.

Do you help with statistics?

We support data cleaning, table preparation, graph development and interpretation guidance based on the study objectives and available data.

Do you reduce plagiarism?

We support original academic rewriting, citation correction and language improvement. We do not support unethical similarity manipulation.
