Academic Integrity, Privacy, Terms and Refund Policy

These policy sections can be reviewed by your legal or business advisor before final publication on the website.

Academic Integrity Policy

SAIL Research Solution provides academic support services such as research guidance, editing, formatting, documentation, statistical interpretation and publication readiness support. We do not support fabricated data, false authorship, ghost research claims, guaranteed publication, unethical similarity manipulation or misuse of institutional resources.

Clients are responsible for the authenticity of data, ethical approval, institutional permission, authorship eligibility, copyright permissions and final submission decisions.

Privacy Policy

Documents, manuscripts, data files and personal details shared by clients are handled confidentially and used only for the requested service. We do not intentionally share client files with unauthorized third parties.

Clients should avoid sharing unnecessary sensitive personal information unless it is essential for the requested academic service.

Terms and Conditions

  • Service scope, timeline and fee must be confirmed before work begins.
  • Client must provide complete and accurate documents, data and instructions.
  • Any journal decision, institutional approval or academic evaluation is outside our control.
  • Revisions are limited to the agreed scope of work.
  • Final responsibility for submission and use of the document remains with the client.

Refund Policy

Refund eligibility depends on the stage of work completed, service type and agreed scope. Once substantial work has been started or completed, full refunds may not be possible. Any refund request should be raised with clear reason and supporting details.

Website Disclaimer

SAIL Research Solution provides academic research guidance, editing, formatting, statistical interpretation and publication support services. We do not guarantee journal acceptance, publication, indexing, ethical approval, authorship eligibility, academic marks, degree completion or research outcomes. All research data, ethical permissions, institutional approvals, authorship responsibility and final submissions remain the responsibility of the respective authors, investigators, students or institutions.
